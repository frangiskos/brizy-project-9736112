Our Company

Trident Boat Hire pride themselves on the personal touch as they are a small but very professional company. The partnership started the business in 2007 with an entirely new fleet and equipment, and have proved very popular with their clients.

 

The owners of the company has an unparalleled knowledge of the sea and coastline. They had a long history of working in this sector and is a qualified PADI dive master and assistant instructor.

 

The company has been featured in several articles in both English and Greek newspapers, and was recently chosen to appear in a TV film highlighting water sports in Cyprus, supported by the Cyprus Tourist Board.
